ANAIS SRS / FDD UPDATE NOTES

Account Management
- Added Deactivate Account function.
- Added Delete Account function. If an account has linked records, the system deactivates it instead of breaking records.
- Account Management is mainly for viewing accounts and editing only role, branch, status, and contact/supplier details.
- Usernames remain unique and are not edited from Account Management.
- New accounts use auto-generated temporary passwords and are created as Inactive until approved by the Owner.
- Added account filters by branch, role, and status.
- Added contact number, email, and address fields.

Dashboard
- Recent Dashboard transactions now focus on Sales Transactions, not general stock movement type.
- The Dashboard does not include Stock-In records in the recent sales table.
- Recent Sales columns are ordered as Transaction Number, Product, Quantity, Branch, Total, Recorded By, and View Details.
- Date and time are moved to the details/receipt view.

Inventory
- Stock-In and Stock-Out are inside the Inventory / Stock module.
- Added car-part categories such as Engine Parts, Brake System, Suspension, Electrical, Lighting, Tires & Wheels, Interior Accessories, Exterior Accessories, Audio & Electronics, Oils & Fluids, Cleaning Supplies, Tools, and Others.
- Added Barcode / QR Code field for easier product tracking.
- Added Warranty Months for products and Warranty Until for stock-in entries.

Stock-Out
- Added customer ID number field for discount reference, such as Senior/PWD/Student ID.
- Discount amount/percent remains supported.

Reports
- Added Sales History report.

Purchase Orders
- Added payment status, method, amount paid, and remarks.
- Added damaged/wrong-item return recording.
- Auto PO generation now groups low-stock products into one draft PO per supplier. If items are from both branches, the PO branch is set to Both.

- Request Account was moved to the Employee/Staff login tab. Supplier registration remains on the Supplier tab using /anais/register_supplier.php.
